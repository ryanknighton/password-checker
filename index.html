How to make sure your password is secure.

In today's world we have passwords for everything. It might be convienient to have a single password for everything but sometimes the data is just too important to not make a secure password. Below are a few reference sites that might help you when creating your next secure password.

  • Krebs on Security
  • Guide to Password Security
  • 5 Myths on Password Security
  • The Importance of Password Security
  • Create and Maintain Secure Passwords

Tips for creating and keeping a strong password.

  • Don’t post it for everyone to see.
  • Make the password is at least 8 characters long.
  • Use a mixture of uppercase and lowercase letters as well as numbers and symbols.
  • Don’t tell anyone your password.
  • Be sure no one watches when you enter your password.
  • Don’t just use one password.
